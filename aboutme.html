Alice spent the first 18 years of her life living in a rural village near Cambridge.

After finishing her school studies she moved to a ski resort in France for a year, before heading to university in Wales.

After finishing her degree in 2015 she moved to Eidsvoll, Norway.

Here in Norway Alice has fully dived into embracing Norwegian life. Her time in France taught her to ski like a Norwegian. She has SKAM to thank for her Norwegian language skills, even if it did mean at first she misunderstood when was and when was not appropriate to use the word «jævelig».

Alice is unashamedly one of those people who takes photo of her food before she eats it. Any future employer is guaranteed homemade Mince Pies and other delicious British recipes!
